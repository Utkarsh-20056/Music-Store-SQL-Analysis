# Business Insights

This project analyzes the Chinook Music Store database using SQL to answer business-related questions and generate actionable insights.

## Key Business Insights

1. **USA generated the highest overall revenue**, making it the most valuable market for the business.

2. **Prague recorded the highest revenue among all billing cities**, indicating strong customer spending in that location.

3. **Rock is the most popular music genre**, with the highest number of tracks purchased.

4. **The top customer contributed significantly more revenue than the average customer**, highlighting the importance of retaining high-value customers.

5. **A small number of customers account for a large percentage of total sales**, suggesting opportunities for loyalty and reward programs.

6. **Some albums consistently outperform others in sales**, helping identify the most successful music collections.

7. **A few artists generate the majority of revenue**, making them key contributors to business performance.

8. **Invoice analysis shows that customer purchases are spread across multiple countries**, demonstrating an international customer base.

9. **Sales data reveals that customers often purchase multiple tracks within a single invoice**, increasing the average order value.

10. **Window functions such as DENSE_RANK(), RANK(), and ROW_NUMBER() were used to identify top-performing customers, artists, and albums efficiently.**

11. **CTEs and Subqueries simplified complex business analysis**, improving query readability and maintainability.

12. **The project demonstrates how SQL can be used to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights for decision-making.**
